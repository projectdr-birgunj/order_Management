Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12/06/2024

About CuisineMate

CuisineMate is designed for restaurants to streamline role-based tasks for users in four categories: Admin, Chef, Cashier, and Waiter. The app does not require personal data for usage and only collects login credentials for account authentication.

Data We Collect

Login Credentials:

  • Email Address and Password: Collected during sign-up and login to authenticate users.
  • Role Information: Each user is assigned one role: Admin, Chef, Cashier, or Waiter.

We do not collect or request any additional personal or sensitive user data.

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect data solely for:

  • User authentication.
  • Assigning and managing roles within the app.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used for:

  • Authentication: To provide secure access to the app.
  • Role Management: To ensure users have the correct permissions based on their assigned role.

Data Sharing Policy

CuisineMate does not share any user data with third parties, including advertisers, analytics providers, or partners. All user data is stored securely within the app's Firebase backend.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data by implementing:

  • Industry-standard encryption during data transmission and storage.
  • Restricted access to user data within Firebase, ensuring only authorized personnel can access it.

Data Retention and Deletion

Retention: Data is retained as long as a user account is active.
Deletion: Users can request account deletion, which will remove all associated data from our systems. Contact us at the email below to initiate this process.

User Rights

  • Access and Review: Users can access and review their account data within the app.
  • Deletion: Users can delete their accounts and all associated data by contacting our support team.
